Join Coca-Cola and be part of the Great British Spring Clean



The Great British Spring Clean (GBSC) is a campaign run by Keep Britain Tidy with the simple aim of bringing people across the country together to clean up the litter in our towns, villages, countryside and beaches. Coca-Cola is one of the proud supporters of the GBSC 2019, helping to fund its delivery across the whole of the UK.


As well as mobilising our employees to be actively involved in litter clean-ups, we are encouraging our customers, suppliers and delivery partners to get involved and actively promote litter clean-ups in their local communities.


We know that many of our customers are hubs of their communities and have a unique relationship with their customers. Sustainability and recycling is high on the public agenda as is localness and caring for the communities’ that people live in. A Community Clean-up, like the Great British Spring Clean, provides an opportunity to get involved in a good cause.
